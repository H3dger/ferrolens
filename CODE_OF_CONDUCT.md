# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want the `ferrolens` community to be welcoming, respectful, and constructive for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- being respectful in discussion and review
- giving actionable, technical feedback
- assuming good intent while still being honest and precise
- focusing criticism on ideas, code, and behavior rather than people

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- harassment or personal attacks
- discriminatory or exclusionary language
- trolling, intimidation, or bad-faith participation
- publishing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, or pull requests that violate this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting

If you encounter unacceptable behavior, report it privately to the project maintainer.
